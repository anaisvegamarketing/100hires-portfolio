## Research Project — LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

### Why I chose this topic
LinkedIn organic content is one of the most powerful and cost-effective channels for B2B SaaS companies to build trust, generate inbound leads, and shorten sales cycles — without spending on ads.

### Experts Researched
I identified and collected recent content from 10 leading practitioners in this space:

1. **Matt Barker** — Data-driven LinkedIn content for B2B SaaS writers
2. **Richard van der Blom** — LinkedIn algorithm researcher, publishes annual Algorithm Insights Report
3. **Katelyn Bourgoin** — Buyer psychology applied to B2B content strategy
4. **Justin Welsh** — LinkedIn organic growth frameworks for founders
5. **Jasmin Alic** — LinkedIn copywriting and hook writing
6. **Tom Hunt** — B2B organic content strategy, founder of Fame
7. **Sam Browne** — Growing on LinkedIn without paid ads
8. **Thibault Louis** — LinkedIn content systems for B2B SaaS teams
9. **Corey Haines** — Turning LinkedIn content into SaaS pipeline
10. **Eman Ismail** — Using LinkedIn content to improve SaaS onboarding

### Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md` — Full expert list with links and annotations
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` — Recent posts from each expert with key insights
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` — Reserved for future transcript collection
- `/research/other/` — Additional research materials

### Key Themes Found Across All Experts
- Consistency and specificity beat volume and broad messaging
- Personal profiles outperform company pages by 5x on LinkedIn
- The first line of every post determines whether anyone reads the rest
- LinkedIn is a trust channel, not a traffic channel — results compound over months
- Teaching your category builds authority before the sales conversation starts
